Zaratan is a boutique coliving owner-operator with roots in California's cooperative movement.

We believe that:
  • Community cannot be bought, only built
  • Home should be a place of safety and freedom
  • Self-governance does not have to be complicated
  • Domestic labor should be recognized and celebrated
  • Housing is not a luxury, and should not be priced as such
  • Caring for our homes is the first step in caring for our planet

We work hard to keep prices low, and in return we ask members to participate in their home's ongoing maintenance. We don't hire house managers, nor do we import domestic labor. We know meetings can be boring and comminication can be hard, so we've developed systems to make participation as fun and easy as playing a game. For those who don't mind the responsibility, we promise that this will be one of the best housing experiences of your life.
