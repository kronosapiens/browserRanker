Zaratan is an emerging coliving owner-operator in Los Angeles, with a focus on freedom and accountability.
We believe that:

Housing is not a luxury, and shouldn't be priced as such – Home should be a place of safety and freedom – Community can't be bought, only built – House Rules don't have to be complicated – Domestic labor should be visible – Caring for our homes leads to caring for our planet.

We work hard to keep prices reasonable, and in return ask members to help with chores – we don't hire house managers or invisible servants. Meetings can be boring, so we've developed tools to make running a house as fun and easy as playing a game. If you don't mind the responsibility, this will be one of the best housing experiences of your life.
