Zaratan is a boutique coliving owner-operator with roots in California's cooperative movement.

We believe that:

Housing is not a luxury, and should not be priced as such – Home should be a place of safety and freedom – Community cannot be bought, only built – Governance does not have to be complicated – Domestic labor should be recognized and celebrated – Caring for our homes is the first step in caring for our planet.

We work hard to keep prices low, and in return members participate in their home's ongoing maintenance. We don't hire house managers or invisible servants. Meetings can be boring, so we've developed systems to make running the house as fun and easy as playing a game. If you don't mind the accountability, this will be one of the best housing experiences of your life.
